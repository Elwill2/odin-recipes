Lasagna Recipe

A lasagna is an italian dish made with tomatoes and meet.

It is a hot dish. You can now find it everywhere in the world.

Ingredients

  • 500g of tomato sauce
  • 300g of meet
  • 100g of cheese
  • bechamel

Steps

  1. Cook the tomatoes
  2. Cook the meet
  3. Prepare the bechamel
  4. Put a layer with each ingredients
  5. Add the cheese
  6. Put it in the hoven
