Burger Recipe

A burger is a sandwich with two buns. In between you can add all the ingredients you like.

It is considered as a fast-food and so you shouldn't eat to much of it.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • salade
  • tomato
  • fried oignons
  • veggi steak

Steps

  1. Cook the veggi steak
  2. Heat up the buns
  3. Once the buns are a bit creespy get them out of the oven
  4. Add all the ingredients
